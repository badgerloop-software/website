Who we are

- University of Wisconsin-Madison students

- Multidisciplinary majors

- Freshmen to PhD students


What we do

- Build pods to compete in Hyperloop Competitions

- Create an unparalleled learning environment for students to work on real world problems in a pseudo-startup setting

- Analyze the feasibility of Hyperloop technology across the U.S.


Why we do it

- Enthused by the concept of Hyperloop technology

- Love to work on challenging problems that could improve the speed and sustainability of future transportation

- Looking to grow as students by applying our knowledge outside the classroom and conquering real-world challenges
