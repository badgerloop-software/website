Founded in 2015, Badgerloop is a student organization at the University of Wisconsin at Madison striving to make the fifth mode of transportation a reality. We provide members with the unique opportunity to work on real world engineering and business challenges during their college years. Our members come from a variety of majors who range from freshmen to PhD students. We compete with teams from around the world in the SpaceX Hyperloop Pod Competition held in Hawthorne, CA.

At Design Weekend in 2016, we took third place out of 120 teams. In January, we competed at Competition I and took home one of two Innovation Awards. We are currently creating an light and fast pod to compete in Competition II in hopes to establish Badgerloop as a leader in the development of Hyperloop technology!
