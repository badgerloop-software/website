Contributing to the development of Hyperloop technology




Who we are

- University of Wisconsin-Madison students

- Multidisciplinary majors

- Freshmen to PhD students

What we do

- Build pods to compete in SpaceX Hyperloop Pod Competitions

- Analyze the feasibility of a nationwide Hyperloop network

- Create an unparalleled learning environment for students to
work on real world problems in a pseudo-startup setting

Why we do it

- Enthusiastic about the concept of Hyperloop technology

- Love to work on challenging problems that could improve
speed and sustainability of future transportation

- Grow as students by taking our learning outside the
classroom and conquering real-world challenges
